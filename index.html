What Makes Opticorder Revolutionary?

Opticorder combines cutting-edge integrated photonics with open source collaboration to create a platform that achieves laboratory-grade performance in portable, field-deployable form factors. By integrating frequency comb generation directly on photonic integrated circuits (PICs), we eliminate the need for complex external stabilization while maintaining unprecedented precision.

Advanced Technology Architecture

Opticorder leverages the latest advances in integrated photonics, combining thin-film lithium niobate (TFLN) and silicon nitride (SiN) platforms for maximum flexibility and performance.

🔬 Primary Platform: TFLN

Thin-Film Lithium Niobate provides strong electro-optic effects for dynamic control, Kerr nonlinearity for broadband comb generation, and integrated stabilization and feedback loops.

Electro-optic Coefficient r33 = 30 pm/V
Nonlinear Index n2 = 2.5×10⁻¹⁹ m²/W
Transparency Range 350nm - 5μm

💎 Alternative Platform: SiN

Silicon Nitride offers ultra-low propagation losses, excellent thermal stability, and seamless integration with optical phased arrays for advanced beamforming applications.

Propagation Loss <0.1 dB/cm
Thermal Stability dn/dT = 2.4×10⁻⁵ K⁻¹
Nonlinear Index n2 = 2.4×10⁻¹⁹ m²/W

📊 Performance Specifications

Spectral Span ≥1.5 Octaves
Timing Jitter ≤30 fs
CEO Stability ≤50 mrad
Power Consumption ≤15 W
Form Factor <1L volume, <3kg weight

⚙️ Configuration Presets

Opticorder offers four specialized configurations optimized for different applications:

MTR-A (Metrology) 100-500 MHz, Er-fiber + PPLN
TEL-G (Telecom) 10-50 GHz, EO-comb on CW seed
KERR-H (LiDAR) 20-40 GHz, SiN Kerr microresonator
DCS-P (Spectroscopy) 100 MHz-10 GHz, Dual matched combs
